About Ulos

Ulos, in the Batak language, means ‘blanket’ or ‘covering cloth,’ yet its significance extends far beyond mere fabric. For the Batak people, ulos represents a symbol of love, warmth, and protection given from heart to heart. Every thread woven carries prayers, hopes, and ancestral heritage that has been passed down through generations for centuries. In Batak philosophy, ulos is not merely ordinary cloth—it is ’tondi’ or soul that provides spiritual warmth. When someone receives ulos, they receive more than just fabric; they receive blessings, protection, and genuine love. Each motif and color in ulos holds profound meaning—from Ulos Ragi Hotang symbolizing fertility, to Ulos Sadum bringing joy and celebration. ICONIC exists to preserve this sacred heritage by reviving the beauty of ulos through contemporary design that continues to honor traditional values. We believe that ulos belongs not only to the past, but also to the future—serving as a bridge connecting generations in the enduring warmth of Batak culture. ‘Ulos ni tondi, ulos ni roha’—Ulos is soul, ulos is heart. This is the spirit we carry in every creation, bringing warmth and meaning to every thread we weave into modern fashion.
