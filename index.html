About Iconic

Founded with a passion for preserving and celebrating Indonesian cultural heritage, Iconic represents the perfect fusion of traditional artistry and contemporary design. We specialize in transforming the timeless beauty of Ulos traditional fabric into modern, wearable art that honors our ancestors while embracing the future.
